04 About

I design foundational product experiences — the kind that shape how teams build and how users grow.

For the past 8 years, I've been working at the intersection of education and technology — mostly at OpenClassrooms, where I joined early and helped scale the product from a small platform to a learning experience reaching 650K+ students.

Along the way, I designed AI-powered learning tools, led strategic UX overhauls, built a design system from scratch, and worked closely with product and engineering to ship things that actually moved the metrics — and the people behind them.

I also spent time at Pass Culture, redesigning the venue experience for one of France's most ambitious cultural apps.

Outside of product, I practice scientific illustration — naturalist drawings of wildlife, botany, and the beauty of living things. It keeps my eye sharp, my hand patient, and reminds me that the best design decisions are often the most invisible ones.

I'm based in Paris, curious about everything, and currently open to founding designer or staff-level roles where I can shape both the product and the culture around it.
